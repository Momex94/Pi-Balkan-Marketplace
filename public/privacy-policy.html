Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2025

Balkan Pi Marketplace (BPM) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in connection with our decentralized marketplace operating within the Pi Network ecosystem.

Information We Collect

  • Pi Network Login Data: Your unique Pi username and public information authorized via the Pi SDK.
  • Device Metadata: Such as language settings, timezone, and basic device details for improving user experience.
  • Usage Data: Interactions within the BPM app (e.g., clicks, listings, payments) to optimize the platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Authenticate users via Pi Login
  • Enable seamless in-app transactions via Pi Pay
  • Enhance user experience and fix bugs
  • Provide support when needed

Data Security

All user data is transmitted securely using HTTPS. We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties.

User Rights

Users may request deletion of their account data by contacting us through the in-app feedback or official email.

Third-Party Services

BPM does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. All features operate within the Pi Network framework.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at balkanpimarketplace@gmail.com.
